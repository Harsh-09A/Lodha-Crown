Welcome to Lodha Crown Dombivli

A tranquil life awaits you at Lodha Crown, a residential gated community in Dombivli. The project has newly launched a new land parcel with fabulous 1 & 2 BHK apartments with efficiently planned living spaces. Dotted with lovely greens, a clubhouse, a Ganesh temple, an outdoor sports court, 3 tier security, and other amenities - the complex is curated to provide easy comforts.

Based in a peaceful corner of Dombivli, the complex is near reputed schools and hospitals such as Eva World School and Aims Hospital. It also offers a dedicated bus service to Dombivli Station, every 20 minutes. You also get quick travel times across Mumbai via the Kalyan-Shil Road & Airoli Katai Road. An upcoming metro station is close by as well. Overall, Lodha Crown is a hidden gem in Dombivli, where you can experience the best of city living yet enjoy adventurous excursions into nature.
